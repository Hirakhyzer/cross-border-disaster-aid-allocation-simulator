# Humanitarian Ethics and Deployment Policy

This repository is a synthetic research prototype. It must not be used to automate or replace humanitarian judgment.

## Non-negotiable boundaries

- Do not treat generated allocations as official aid orders.
- Do not use synthetic scores as moral rankings of communities.
- Do not use route outputs as operational navigation or border instructions.
- Do not upload sensitive humanitarian datasets without authorization.
- Do not deploy with real people affected by crisis without accountable governance.

## Required review before real data

Real use requires humanitarian partner approval, legal review, privacy review, data-protection controls, field validation, local-context review, accessibility checks, and human accountability.

## Fairness caution

Fairness metrics can reveal disparities, but they cannot decide what is just. They should trigger review, not automate final decisions.
